About

This is an effort to provide a platform to the farmers of our nation to be able to sell their produce online, hence cutting out middlemen and increasing their income.

Buyers pan-India, be it individual consumers or corporations can buy crops from all across India, creating India's first truly nationwide agriculture platform.

Since our platform is farmer centric, we also provide diagnostic tools to farmers through which they can take well informed, intelligent decisions on when to sell their crop in the marketplace. This helps them decide on investments in cold storage solutions and assists in maximizing overall profits.

This is a project by sophomore students of Computer Science and Engineering branch of the Indian Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, undertaken in the guidance of Professor S.K. Singh.
